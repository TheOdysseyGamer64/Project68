Campus Life

School PE - Rugby
Wilmington Grammar School was established as WGSB in 1954

It was known as Dartford Technical School until 1954, where it was renamed to be WGSB



School Library

Was created in 1954 and has since then gone through various renovations to suit the modern style.

The Library boasts over 8000 books with different genres and levels. This will continue to grow as more students enter WGSB.



School Assembly Hall

Was built in 1954

The hall consists of 1080 seats and is also used as a dining hall.
